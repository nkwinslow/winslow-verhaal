We pride ourselves in coming up with unique fixturing solutions that enable long spindle run times and repeatable setups. We utilize the latest fixturing solutions in the industry, and even come up with our own when all others fail to deliver the flexibility and durability we need.

Camworks is our CAM system of choice, and integrates well within Solidworks. Camworks is feature based machining at it's finest. Instead of wasting time programming every feature like other CAM software such as Mastercam, Camworks finds the features and applies Winslow-Verhaals' own unique machining strategies in just a few clicks. This saves countless hours of programming time with less programming errors. Camworks allows us to utilize High-Speed and High-Efficiency toolpaths to produce parts with the shortest cycle times possible.
